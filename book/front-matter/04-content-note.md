# Content Note

This book contains direct discussion of childhood trauma, family violence, emotional neglect, addiction, relapse, grief, death, suicidal thoughts, legal consequences, shame, Complex PTSD, strained parent-child relationships, and the long-term effects of survival patterns.

It is written from lived experience. Some passages may be difficult to read, especially for readers with trauma histories, addiction histories, grief, family estrangement, or legal-system trauma.

Please read at your own pace.

Put the book down when you need to.

Come back when your body says it is safe enough.

If you are in immediate danger or feel at risk of harming yourself or someone else, contact emergency services or a crisis support line in your area.
