# Memoir Disclaimer

This book is a memoir.

It is a record of memory, lived experience, interpretation, reflection, and meaning-making after the fact. It is not a court record, clinical report, investigative document, medical file, or legal filing.

The author writes from his own perspective and does not claim to speak for every person named, referenced, affected, or involved.

Where other people appear in the story, they are included because they shaped the author’s lived experience, memory, consequences, relationships, or recovery. Their inclusion is not meant to reduce them to one moment, one conflict, one role, or one version of themselves.

Some names, identifying details, scenes, dialogue, timelines, and descriptions may be changed, condensed, or reconstructed from memory for privacy, clarity, narrative flow, or safety.

This book is not an excuse for harm.

It is an attempt to tell the truth without hiding the machinery that made the harm possible.
